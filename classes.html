NO JUDGEMENT POLICY:

We are passionate about the importance of both spiritual and physical health. Please also note that Dawn Rising is a JUDGEMENT FREE zone! If you have not already accepted Christ, we will not push (this excludes class directed speech during instruction time), nor will your peers. If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to us during regular office hours. If your concern regards an instuctor or class peer, please reach out to the studio director BY EMAIL and label the subject line as "URGENT!private" and leave as much detail as possible in the message box.
